Stream

Stream is an iOS accounting app developed on Xcode. The purpose of this app is to allow users to record every spend, and at the end of each month, users can find out where the money is spent.

In the main interface of the app, users can see all recent expenses and income. Users can add new consumption records through the buttons at the bottom of the screen. Users can also leave comments for different consumption records, and customize the category for the app.

Stream can analyze the percentage of income or expenses for users. And users can view this information according to different months or years.

Finally, the stream also has an achievement feature. When the user reaches a specific condition, the corresponding achievement will become coloured. In order to motivate the user to maintain good consumption habits.
