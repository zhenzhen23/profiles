Bomber Girl

The Bomber Girl was recreated by Unity 3d engine to commemorate the classic game "Bomberman".

The goal of the game is to manipulate the girl to use bombs to defeat all enemies within the specified time and find the door to the next level.

Player can randomly get different props by blowing up walls or trees. These props can strengthen the player's abilities, such as increasing the range of the bomb, increasing the speed of movement, etc.

When player defeats all enemies and passes through the door, player will enter a more complex and challenging level
