Flash - In development

Main Idea

Our idea is an errand app called “Flash” to assist people to complete tasks that they do not have time to do. The errand app will source highly rated service for overwhelmed/busy professionals customers to free up their time.

Use Case diagram

After the installation of the application, the user will be required to sign up for an account. Signing up requires a valid email, phone number and home address. In addition, payment information must be provided for automatically charging for the services used.


When the user comes across a task that needs to be done around the house but finds that they don't have the time to complete it. Then they can list it on the app. The listing requires a description of the task, the estimated time the user wished it to be done. After listing the task, the information goes public to Task Runners and they can accept your task for the list. When a Runner shows interest in your task you have a chance to screen them based on user reviews before accepting them. After accepting, the two parties can make arrangements to begin the task. The price will depend on the distance of the task or different types of task. Full payment is not released to the Runner until the task is complete.

The value gained from the app

The app will provide valuable services to our valued customer, the customers will gain some values from our services. For example,


- Time-saving


- Improve efficiency


- Reliable, punctual and conscientious service


- High-quality services from our product


- Reasonable prices of our service
